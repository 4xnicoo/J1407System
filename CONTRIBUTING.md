# Contributing
Thank you for your interest in contributing to **J1407 System**! Contributions from the community are welcome and appreciated.

### Guidelines

- Please keep commits clear and descriptive.
- Avoid committing temporary files, logs, or IDE cache.

### Licensing

By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the same terms as the main project:

- **Code and configuration files:** CC-BY 4.0  
- **Art and assets:** CC-BY 4.0  

This ensures your work can be freely shared, modified, and redistributed by others, with attribution.
More information on "LICENSE" file.

### Attribution

All contributors will be credited in the project README or a dedicated contributors list.

---

We really appreciate your help! If you have ideas or suggestions, feel free to open an issue or a pull request.
